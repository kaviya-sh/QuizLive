# Can't Login? Here's How to Fix It

If you're having trouble logging into your sparklo.in account, follow these simple steps:

## Quick Fix: Reset Your Password

1. **Go to the login page**: https://sparklo-in.vercel.app/login

2. **Click "Forgot password?"** link below the password field

3. **Enter your email address** and click "Send OTP"

4. **Check your email** for a 6-digit code (check spam folder too!)

5. **Enter the code** on the website

6. **Create a new password** (minimum 6 characters)

7. **Login with your new password**

That's it! Your login should work now.
